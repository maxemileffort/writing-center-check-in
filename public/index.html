Tips and Hints

Some information to get you started...

Students:

Arriving students should go to the student area. From there, you can login in if you have been here already. If this is the first visit, then you can scroll to the bottom of the page and click "Register".

Registering lets us track whether or not you have been here and what has been discussed, in the event your teacher decides to reward students seeking help on their own.

Logging in lets us keep track of what order students arrive. Please, login promptly on each arrival so we can better know in what order to help everyone.
